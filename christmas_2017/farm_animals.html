FARM ANIMALS

Countless Haitian families go to bed hungry every night. They wake not knowing where their next meal will come from. Stomachs ache and bodies wither. In time, these families’ hopes succumb to a daily, overwhelming despair.

Father Glenn Meaux has a heart for Haiti’s poor, and he’s working to change their lives and end this hunger. This year, he needs your help to supply poor subsistence farmers with a practical, renewable resource that fits their rural way of life: livestock.

Your gift through Cross Catholic Outreach will bless a needy family with a cattle — robust animals families can breed for long term food and income. The recipients are then shown how to “pay it forward” by giving back one of the offspring to be raised by another poor family.

A $30 gift can provide veterinary treatment for this animal Husbandry program. In rural Haiti, a meal of rice and beans will feed a family for a day, but a gift of a farm animal can help sustain them for the long run
