Life-Giving Water

This Christmas help repair a community water system that will pump clean, abundant water to the Batey community of Las Frias, as well as other surrounding communities. These communities currently rely on contaminated water sources for survival, which wreaks havoc on the health of the families and children in this region.

Your gift can provide 375 families access to clean, potable water, to improve health, hydration, sanitation, and general quality of life. When complete, the project will service approximately 800 adults and 500 children. A gift to this special project will improve the health and well-being of thousands of families who suffer from waterborne diseases. This Christmas, give the gift of water!
