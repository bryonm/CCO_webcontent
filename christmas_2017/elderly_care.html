ELDERLY CARE

At the St. Martin’s home for the aged, Christ’s love is felt around every corner. This special project responds to the need of a great number of elderly people living in the countryside who do not have family or other caregivers to take care of them. The home provides care and support to the elderly, meeting all physical needs such as food, medication and hygiene. To achieve optimum care, The Diocese and the Morains feed the residents three hearty meals each day. They maintain a pleasant, sanitary environment by keeping hygiene and cleaning supplies on hand. They ensure the staff members who care for the daily needs of the residents are paid adequately and on time. And like them, Cross Catholic is devoted to the well-being of the elderly in Grenada — but we need your commitment as well. Help us supply this nursing home materially and spiritually, allowing each resident to know that Christ will carry them through their golden years.
