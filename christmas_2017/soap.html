SOAP

We rarely think of soap as a critical health item, but for families living in dirt-floor shacks with no plumbing and no modern sanitary products, a bar of soap may be their only line of defense against parasites and deadly diseases. A $6 gift can send about a year’s supply of soap to a person in need. Soap is a holiday gift that will put smiles on the faces of the poor and evoke tremendous gratitude. They will enjoy peace-of-mind, knowing that because of your gift, their children will no longer be defenseless against infections and contaminants. This commonsense preventative measure also spares families from medical bills they simply cannot afford.

SOAP FOR DISEASE PREVENTION

Send a family soap to help prevent the spread of parasites and deadly diseases.
